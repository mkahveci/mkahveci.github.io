Teaching Philosophy

My teaching is grounded in constructivist principles, creating interactive and inquiry-based environments that foster both cognitive understanding and affective growth.

Active & Collaborative Learning

I emphasize students' prior experiences and active engagement through collaborative activities and hands-on projects to build deep conceptual connections.

Inquiry-Based Strategies

By equipping students with critical thinking skills, I empower them to see the relevance of chemistry in real-world societal and personal contexts.

Technology & Innovation

I develop and employ evidence-based tools, such as diagnostic assessments and interactive modules, to help students overcome conceptual difficulties.

Online Teaching Initiative
To facilitate online learning, I have created a dedicated resource: iLearnChem: Interactive Chemistry Understanding (.icu). Visit kahveci.icu

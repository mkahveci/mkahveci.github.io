## Course Description
This graduate-level course provides a comprehensive overview of the **theoretical foundations and practical application of qualitative research**. The curriculum delves into the ontological and epistemological underpinnings of various methodologies. Students become familiar with a wide range of approaches, including **naturalistic inquiry, ethnography, grounded theory, and action research**. In addition to exploring the theoretical aspects, the course provides hands-on experience in using qualitative methods for **data collection, interpretation, and reporting**. The goal is to equip students with a deep theoretical understanding and the practical skills necessary to conduct rigorous qualitative research projects.

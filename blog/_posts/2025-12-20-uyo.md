I am thrilled to announce the official release of **TradeTrack Pro v1.0.0**.

For too long, retail traders have been stuck manually updating messy spreadsheets or paying monthly subscriptions for bloated software that doesn't actually help them *improve*. TradeTrack Pro changes that dynamic today.

I built this not just as a log, but as a **smart portfolio management system** designed to enforce discipline, track mechanics, and accelerate your growth as a trader.

## 🎯 Why TradeTrack Pro?

Most journals just record what happened. TradeTrack Pro tells you **how you performed** against professional standards.

### 1. Built-In "Elite Coach" Intelligence
At the heart of v1.0.0 is the **AI Trading Coach**. This isn't a generic chatbot. I strictly prompted this intelligence engine to train on **TastyTrade mechanics** and professional risk management rules.

With a single click of the **"Ask AI"** button, the system analyzes your entire trade history for a specific group and provides:
* **Mechanics Check:** Did you sell premium in high IV Rank? Did you manage at 21 DTE?
* **Rule Enforcement:** Are you hitting the 30% Annualized ROC target?
* **Actionable Advice:** A bold, rationale-backed instruction (e.g., **HOLD & MONITOR** or **ROLL UNTESTED SIDE**).

### 2. Intelligent Trade Grouping
Options traders know that a single "Iron Condor" is actually four different legs that might be rolled or adjusted at different times. TradeTrack Pro understands **Strategy Grouping**. You can add legs to existing trades, roll positions, and track the **Net P&L** of the entire campaign—not just isolated transactions.

### 3. Frictionless Workflow
I designed the interface for speed:
* **Smart Duplication:** Clone complex legs (Strategy, Strike, Expiry, IVR, PoP) instantly to enter the other side of a spread.
* **Auto-Calculations:** Type your fill price and quantity, and watch the cost basis and fees update automatically.
* **Dark Mode Native:** A sleek, high-contrast interface designed for late-night research sessions.

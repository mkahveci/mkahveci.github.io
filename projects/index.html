Projects

Our projects reflect a dedication to advancing education, fostering innovation, and addressing critical challenges in science and chemistry education. Rooted in a commitment to bridging research and practice, we have led initiatives that range from investigating artificial intelligence in academic integrity to developing alternative assessment tools for conceptual understanding in biochemistry. Through our work on pedagogical discontentment and self-efficacy, we contribute to global education reform and cross-cultural validation efforts.

Each project underscores our mission to empower educators, inspire learners, and create lasting impacts on STEM education. At the Kahveci Group, we are driven to innovate, collaborate, and create solutions that resonate across diverse educational contexts. These efforts are part of our larger vision to support equitable learning environments and to inspire meaningful advancements in teaching and learning through evidence-based practices.

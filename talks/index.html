Talks

Our team actively shares a passion for chemistry education by delivering engaging presentations at conferences, workshops, and seminars. Our talks cover a wide range of topics including innovative teaching strategies, the integration of technology in the classroom, and the creation of inclusive learning environments. We are particularly interested in discussing our research on affective dimensions in chemistry education, alternative assessment methods, and the use of technology to enhance student learning. We also enjoy sharing insights from our experience co-editing the book "Affective Dimensions in Chemistry Education." Our goal as speakers is to inspire fellow educators and contribute to the ongoing dialogue about effective teaching practices in STEM fields.
